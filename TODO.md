Camera Placement Angles
Add camera angles (maximum 6 angles):

- If the ID of the selected room is available, add camera angles around that room.
- 6 angles include: 2 cameras perpendicular to the top and bottom edges, 2 cameras perpendicular to the two side edges; 2 cameras at a 45-degree angle.
- In the Camera Placement Angles section, provide arrows to change camera angles, and add a checkmark to select an angle (checked angles will be sent to the API for visualization).
- When changing camera angles, the correct camera angle currently set on the minimap and the viewing direction must be displayed.
- The default FOV will be 60-70.
- Capture and send multiple images from those camera angles to the API to receive multiple visualization images.
- Reset the camera angles if the user exits "AI image creation" mode.
